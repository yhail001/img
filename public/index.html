Research Investigations

AHA 2024: Prolonged Fluoroquinolone Use and Risk of Valvular Abnormalities and Abdominal Aortic Aneurysm

We hypothesized that prolonged fluoroquinolone use may increase the risk of valvular abnormalities and abdominal aortic aneurysm by impairing collagen synthesis and promoting tissue degradation.

Comparative Efficacy of Pomalidomide vs. Teclistamab in Preventing AL Amyloidosis Progression in Refractory Multiple Myeloma.

This study compares the efficacy of Pomalidomide versus Teclistamab in preventing the progression of AL amyloidosis among patients with Multiple Myeloma.

Severe Cutaneous Reaction Induced by Clindamycin: A Case Report of Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis

We report a case of clindamycin-induced TEN following the recent administration of Clindamycin for a developing sacral decubitus ulcer.
